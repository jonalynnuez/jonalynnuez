About Me


They say everyone in this world is unique, maybe with their physical appearance or their personality, and everyone like their uniqueness
but I don't like mine. Yes, I am a responsible and empathic person however I am a dependent person as well, I cannot do things all on my
own and that trait of mine is my hatest. I am Jonalyn Nuez, a twenty-year-old student currently enrolled at Isabela State University-Ilagan
Campus pursuing a degree in Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Mangcuram City of Ilagan, Isabela. My father is a
farmer, and my mother is now in paradise with the Almighty God, I am their firstborn, and I have a younger sister we are only 1 year
apart she is studying at Isabela State University- Cauayan Campus pursuing a degree in Bachelor of Science in Elementary Education.
My hobbies are reading Wattpad stories, watching Kdrama, and playing online games. Reading Wattpad stories gives me peace
of mind and improves my spelling. I love reading Wattpad stories because it is a great thing to do, and imagination is better than reality.
Another hobby of mine is watching K-Dramas. It gives me a way to treat my stress and depression, and my one more hobby is playing
online games because it gives me an immersive experience and requires strategy and problem-solving skills to win a game.
Whoever I am today is because of my family. At every point in my life, I always receive their motivation. They encourage me even if I fail
sometimes. I am a responsible person and I do what I am supposed to do, not because I want to but because needed to because I am the eldest.
As of right now, I am a student, so I complete my homework every day and prepare for my classes. Most of the time, I pay attention in my
online class. Along with it, I also organize my job. I also help my parents because with household chores like doing laundry and
washing dishes. Because it is my duty as the eldest daughter to do so, I also tidy my room once a week. When my friends and
classmates need aid, I assist them as well. I give out advice and suggestions to them when they need support and help as well. Generally,
I can say that I am a responsible person who does my duties. Besides being a responsible person, I am also an empathic person.
Because I'm very curious about the life of people around me. I try to learn a lot about people so, in this way, I study their characters
before I react. I know that I am not perfect and I never try to be but one thing is true I am who I am. Even in my school days, I was always
motivated by my friends to discover also myself. So, I can say that I am pretty satisfied with the people surrounding me and I'm grateful
to God for giving me a supportive ambiance altogether in my life. Finding out what life genuinely has in store for you is more
important than always whining about what you haven't received from it. Every person has potential, and it is up to us how we use it.
We weave the past into the future to create a lovely pattern. I constantly look forward to the freedom warriors as my role models and aspire
to be like them. My motivation comes from the zeal, vigor, and vitality with which they overcame obstacles in their lives and improved
the world as we know it now. Therefore, how we master ourselves will determine how wonderful our future will be,
and I believe this. I wish all of my pals the best of luck.
